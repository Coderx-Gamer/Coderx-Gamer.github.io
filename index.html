About

UI-Utils is a FabricMC Minecraft modification which adds multiple features to Minecraft GUI(s) which are useful for debugging plugins and glitch hunting on servers. This mod is always updated to the latest version of Minecraft (but not snapshots or pre-releases.) This mod works on most Linux distributions and Windows, however, Windows isn't frequently tested and OSX isn't tested at all (OSX may not work,) most of the releases are tested on Linux.
