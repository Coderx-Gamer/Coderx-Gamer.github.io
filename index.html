About

UI-Utils is a FabricMC Minecraft modification that adds multiple features to Minecraft GUI(s,) making it useful for debugging plugins and glitch hunting on servers. This mod is always updated to the latest version of Minecraft (but not snapshots or pre-releases.) While it primarily works on most Linux distributions, Windows compatibility is not frequently tested, and OSX compatibility is untested (OSX may not work.) Most of the releases are tested on Linux.
